The tokenized data eg `your_train_data.bin` and `your_val_data.bin` should be in this folder.

To obtain these, you should use a pre-trained tokenizer (or train your own one) to tokenize your txt training data.

If you want to train a tokenizer from scratch, welcome to check my another repo https://github.com/Siyuan-Harry/bpe-optimized-from-scratch.
